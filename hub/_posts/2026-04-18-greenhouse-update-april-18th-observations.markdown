Our greenhouse recorded a steady stream of data on April 18th, offering a clear look at the microclimate our herbs are enjoying. Overall, the environment remained quite stable, with temperatures holding nicely in the low 20s Celsius.

Ambient humidity saw a slight dip throughout the day, moving from a high of 42.2% down to 40.5%. Meanwhile, light levels progressed nicely, starting lower and building up to a peak of 943.3 lux by the afternoon.

Soil moisture levels for Basil, Chilli, and Oregano all look well-hydrated. Basil maintained its content around 89%, Chilli stayed steady near 83%, and Oregano was at about 80%. These readings suggest our watering schedule is keeping everything comfortably moist.

Individual plant temperatures were also consistent. Oregano showed a slight cooling trend, ending the day at 19.94°C, while Basil and Chilli maintained very stable readings throughout the monitoring period. It looks like a perfect day for growth!
