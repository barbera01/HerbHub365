It has been a bright and productive day at Herb Hub 365. Light levels peaked at a vibrant 1448.3 lux during the height of the afternoon before settling to a quiet 20 lux as evening approached. The greenhouse stayed comfortably warm for our plants, averaging 24.54°C with a peak of 26.13°C, providing a steady environment for growth.

We observed several notable shifts in the atmosphere today. Humidity peaked at 38.7% before dipping to a low of 25.5% and eventually stabilizing at 31.9%. Atmospheric pressure followed a similar gentle downward trend, moving from 1021 hPa in the morning to 1018 hPa by late afternoon.

Our plants have been drawing steadily from their soil reserves. Moisture levels for the Oregano and Chilli dipped slightly to 53.58% and 47.76% respectively, while the Basil saw the most significant change, moving from 48% down to 42.93% by the end of the day.

The herbs themselves are maintaining healthy temperatures, with the Basil reaching a comfortable high of 24.25°C. Interestingly, our water supply remained the coolest part of the greenhouse, averaging a refreshing 19.69°C. As we head into the evening hours, the greenhouse remains stable and well-positioned for the night.
