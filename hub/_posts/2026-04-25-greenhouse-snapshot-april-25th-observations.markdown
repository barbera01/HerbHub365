Good morning, green thumbs! Here is a look at how things were doing in the Herb Hub 365 greenhouse yesterday, April 25th. Overall, the environment remained quite stable throughout the recorded hours.

Ambient conditions showed a gentle warming trend, with the temperature climbing from a low of 19.94°C up to 20.4°C. Light levels also increased steadily, peaking at 198.3 lux by the end of the monitoring period. Humidity stayed consistently low, hovering around the 39% mark.

For our herbs, temperatures were quite consistent across the board. Basil and Oregano maintained temperatures around 19.6°C, while Chilli held steady at 19.5°C. The water reservoir temperature tracked a slight dip, finishing near 18.9°C.

Soil moisture showed gradual changes. Basil's level dipped slightly from 38.36% to 38.17%, while Chilli remained robustly hydrated at 52.63%. Oregano also saw a slight decrease, moving from 49.37% to 49.11%.

Water reservoir readings were not available for this snapshot, so we will keep an eye on those levels tomorrow! Everything appears to be tracking nicely, and the herbs seem content in their stable environment. Happy growing!
