The temperature started at a comfortable 19.7°C and gradually warmed as the day progressed, reaching a peak of 21.9°C by 8:30 AM. Our herbs are settling into their routine with steady, healthy readings as the environment stabilizes.

The basil is showing a slight decline in soil moisture, dropping from 37.06% to 36.38% over the hour, which is a normal part of its daily cycle. Meanwhile, the chilli plant remains well-hydrated at a robust 77% soil moisture, while the oregano sits comfortably in the middle at roughly 50%.

The water reservoir has been holding its ground, maintaining a stable volume throughout the monitoring window. There were no alerts or warnings triggered by the sensors, and the atmospheric pressure remained consistent at 1012 hPa. It has been a calm and productive start to the day, with all systems functioning smoothly for our growing team.
