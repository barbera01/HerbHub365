Hello Herb Gardeners! We've monitored the greenhouse environment over the last few hours, and things appear quite stable as we head into the afternoon. The ambient temperature saw a nice climb, peaking at 21.87°C, and light levels remained strong, reaching up to 735.8 lux.

Humidity has been trending downward throughout the monitoring period, moving from a high of 43.7% down to 33.1%. This slight drop is normal as the afternoon progresses and the plants continue their photosynthesis work.

Our herbs are showing consistent readings across the board. The basil, chili, and oregano temperatures rose steadily, with the basil reaching its highest recorded temperature of 19.56°C. The water temperature followed a similar upward trend.

Soil moisture levels also showed a gradual, expected decline for all three varieties. The chili remains the most consistently moist, hovering around 65% soil moisture. Overall, the environment seems well-nurtured with good light exposure and moderate temperature swings. Keep monitoring those moisture levels, but for now, everything looks wonderful in the Herb Hub 365!
