The greenhouse experienced a steady and active morning, capturing readings from early hours until mid-morning. Overall, the environment saw a gradual increase in light levels, peaking near 1000 lux by the final reading, which suggests a bright day ahead for our herbs.

Temperatures showed a pleasant warming trend throughout the monitoring period. The ambient temperature started around 17.16°C and climbed to 19.07°C by the time the final reading was taken. Individual plants tracked this gentle rise, with basil reaching its highest recorded temperature near the end of the observation window.

Moisture levels remained healthy across the board. Basil maintained a strong reading, ending at 65.12%, while oregano settled at 58.6%. Chilli’s moisture content showed a slight dip, finishing at 51.39%. These readings suggest the plants are well-hydrated as the day progresses.

Humidity fluctuated nicely, starting at 46.4% and dipping to 41.2% by the last snapshot. This natural shift in humidity, paired with the increasing light, helps maintain good air circulation for optimal growth. We are monitoring these stable conditions closely!
