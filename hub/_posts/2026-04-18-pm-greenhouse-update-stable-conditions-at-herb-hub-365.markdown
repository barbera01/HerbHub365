The afternoon readings from the greenhouse indicate a period of stable, warm conditions. Ambient temperatures peaked nicely around midday, reaching 23.66°C, and light levels were quite strong, hitting a high of 1390 lux.

Humidity naturally fluctuated throughout the monitoring period, ranging from a low of 33.7% to a high of 42.2%. This variation is normal as the greenhouse cycles through its day, and air pressure remained steady, holding around 1015–1017 hPa.

For our herbs, soil moisture levels show a gentle, gradual decrease, which is expected as the plants naturally utilize available water. Basil, Chili, and Oregano all maintained moisture levels within a comfortable range this afternoon.

Overall, the environment appears to be supporting healthy growth! We continue to monitor these metrics closely to ensure everything remains perfectly balanced for our fragrant herbs.
