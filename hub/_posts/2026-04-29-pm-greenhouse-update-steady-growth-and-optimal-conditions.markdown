The Herb Hub 365 greenhouse enjoyed a bright and active afternoon session today, tracking conditions from early morning through peak sunlight hours. Overall, the environment remained stable, with the average temperature climbing nicely throughout the recorded period.

By midday, the ambient temperature reached a high of 23.79°C, accompanied by peak light levels hitting 759.2 lux. Humidity saw a noticeable dip, starting near 52.1% and settling down to a low of 28.6% as the day progressed.

The herbs showed steady activity throughout the afternoon. Basil's soil moisture dipped to 53.79%, while Oregano maintained a healthy level around 60.88%. Chilli's moisture content remained quite consistent, hovering around the mid-50% mark.

Temperatures across the board tracked the ambient rise, with Basil peaking at 20.75°C and Oregano at 20.25°C. The water reservoir temperature also remained high, peaking at 16.937°C.

It seems the plants are responding very well to the bright conditions! We will continue to monitor moisture levels as the cycle continues tomorrow. Stay tuned for more updates from the Hub!
