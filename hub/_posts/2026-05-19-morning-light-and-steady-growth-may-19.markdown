The greenhouse underwent a gentle transition this morning between 6:00 AM and 8:30 AM UTC. Ambient air temperatures rose steadily from a crisp 19.28°C to a peak of 21.6°C. Throughout this window, humidity levels remained comfortably stable, averaging a consistent 47.77%.

Light levels saw the most dramatic shift as the sun climbed higher. Starting at a soft 149.2 lux, sensors recorded a significant surge to 790 lux by the final reading. This rapid increase in brightness provides a vital energy boost for the herbs as they begin their daily photosynthetic cycle.

Soil moisture showed active fluctuations, particularly for the Basil, which peaked at 71.04% before settling at 67.51%. The Chilli plants maintained a steady 56.25% moisture level, while the Oregano remained the driest resident at 41.34%.

Despite the warming air, internal plant temperatures stayed remarkably consistent at approximately 19°C. This closely tracked the water temperature, which reached a high of 19.44°C. With atmospheric pressure holding steady at 1000 hPa, it was a calm and productive start to the day at Herb Hub 365.
