The afternoon observations at Herb Hub 365 show the greenhouse settling into a calm rhythm. By the end of the monitoring period, the average temperature remained comfortable, hovering around the low 20s Celsius.

As the light faded, humidity dipped slightly, finishing the day at an average of 33.3%. Ambient light also decreased steadily, dropping from a high of 1467.5 lux to a minimum of 109.2 lux by the last reading.

Soil moisture levels look stable across the board. Basil finished at 59.14%, Chilli at 67.3%, and Oregano at 66.06%. These readings suggest the plants are holding their hydration nicely as evening approaches.

Individual herb temperatures were consistent throughout the afternoon, with Basil averaging 19.88°C, Chilli at 19.27°C, and Oregano at 19.24°C. The water reservoir temperature also remained steady at an average of 18.3°C.
