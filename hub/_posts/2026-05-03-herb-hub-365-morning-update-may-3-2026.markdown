It was a calm start to the day at Herb Hub 365, with the greenhouse environment remaining comfortable and stable during our early morning monitoring. We are keeping a close watch on these levels to ensure the basil stays healthy through the afternoon.

Individual plant temperatures remained slightly cooler than the surrounding air, with the basil, chilli, and oregano all maintaining consistent readings between 22.06°C and 22.38°C. Meanwhile, the reservoir water temperature was perfectly steady at 22.5°C. Despite the thirsty basil, the greenhouse remains a peaceful and productive environment.
