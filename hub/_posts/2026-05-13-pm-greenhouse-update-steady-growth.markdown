Our herbs have enjoyed a stable afternoon environment today. The average temperature held steady around 20.85°C, with a slight dip toward the end of our monitoring period. Humidity levels remained quite comfortable, averaging 40.4% across the recorded time. Soil moisture readings also showed gentle trends across the board, indicating consistent hydration for our plants.

The temperature readings for both basil and chilli also remained within ideal ranges, showing steady performance across the beds.

Overall, the conditions are perfect for continued healthy growth. All monitoring systems are reporting normal operation, and the water reservoirs are well-stocked. Happy growing to all!
