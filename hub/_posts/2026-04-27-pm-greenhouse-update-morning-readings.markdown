Hello Herb Lovers! Here is your afternoon check-in from the Herb Hub 365 greenhouse. We observed a gentle shift in conditions as the morning progressed, giving us a good look at how our herbs are responding to the environment.

Overall, the ambient temperature remained quite stable during our monitoring window, ranging from a low of 18.14°C to a peak of 20.31°C. Humidity levels also fluctuated nicely, moving from a low of 42.7% to a high of 48%, settling into a comfortable average.

Soil moisture levels showed consistent readings across the board. Basil maintained a steady level, while Chilli and Oregano showed minor dips, which is typical as the morning light increases.

The individual herb temperatures were quite close, suggesting a balanced microclimate. Basil averaged around 19.1°C, Chilli around 19.0°C, and Oregano around 19.1°C.

We continue to monitor the water reservoir, but no changes in water level were recorded. Everything appears to be settling into a routine rhythm, and the plants look happy soaking up the energy from the light!
