This steady climb in illumination suggests the greenhouse is receiving excellent natural light, providing the perfect conditions for photosynthesis as the sun moved higher in the sky.

There were no signs of rapid drying or saturation, indicating that the current watering schedule is keeping the roots happy without any need for immediate intervention.

Overall, the morning session was a smooth operation with no alarms or warnings triggered by the sensors. The pressure remained constant at 1000 hPa, and humidity levels slowly dipped as the air warmed, a normal and expected shift. With the plants thriving and the environment stable, we are well-positioned to continue our growth journey into the afternoon.
