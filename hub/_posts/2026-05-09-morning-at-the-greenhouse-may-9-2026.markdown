Between 6:00 AM and 8:30 AM UTC, light levels at Herb Hub 365 climbed steadily from a soft 243.3 lux to a peak of 507.5 lux. This gentle sunrise provided the perfect start for the daily growth cycle, bathing the herbs in increasing brightness as the morning progressed.

The greenhouse environment warmed comfortably during this window, with ambient temperatures rising from 22.28°C to 23.71°C. Humidity remained crisp and stable, averaging 39.87%, while atmospheric pressure held firm at a consistent 1016 hPa. These steady conditions created a balanced atmosphere for the early hours.

Soil moisture readings showed a natural, slight decline as the herbs began to drink. The oregano remains the most hydrated, moving from 68.15% to 66.42%, while the basil shifted from 57.45% to 55.47%. The chilli plant held steady, finishing the observation at 47.85%, signaling healthy transpiration across the beds.

The plants themselves stayed slightly cooler than the surrounding air, with the basil reaching 22.13°C and the oregano hitting 21.81°C. Meanwhile, the water temperature remained remarkably consistent, hovering right around 21.3°C. It was a calm, productive morning with all systems functioning smoothly.
