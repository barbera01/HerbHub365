Hello Herb Lovers! Here is your afternoon snapshot from the Herb Hub 365 greenhouse. The day offered lovely fluctuations, especially in the light levels as the afternoon unfolded.

Overall, the environment remained quite stable. Temperatures ranged nicely from a low of 19.87°C to a peak of 25.99°C, with the average settling around 22.93°C. Humidity saw a slight dip, ending at 35.1% by the final reading.

Light intensity was most active around midday, reaching a maximum of 1342.5 lux. This provided plenty of energy for our herbs! Soil moisture levels stayed consistent across varieties; Basil dipped slightly to 66.69%, Chilli remained steady around 76.4%, and Oregano finished at 74.19%.

Individual herb temperatures tracked the general trend, peaking for Basil at 23.31°C and Oregano at 22.69°C during the peak light hours. Everything appears to be holding steady through this afternoon cycle. Keep enjoying the beautiful growth here at Herb Hub 365!
