Hello green thumbs! We've gathered the latest data from the greenhouse, offering a snapshot of how our herbs are doing as we move through the day. Overall, conditions appear quite stable for our wonderful collection of basil, chili, and oregano.

The ambient environment showed a slight warming trend, with the temperature climbing from 19.94°C to a peak of 20.4°C. Humidity remained fairly consistent, hovering around 39%, while light levels saw a nice increase, reaching up to 198.3 lux.

Checking in on the plants, soil moisture levels show a steady pattern. Basil maintained its moisture content, dipping slightly. Both chili and oregano showed minor decreases in moisture, settling at 52.63% and 49.11% by the end of the monitoring period, respectively.

Individual pot temperatures were quite consistent, with basil averaging around 19.58°C and oregano holding steady near 19.63°C. We will keep an eye on the water reservoir levels for our next update! Keep enjoying the vibrant growth; we'll continue monitoring everything closely here at Herb Hub 365. Happy gardening!
