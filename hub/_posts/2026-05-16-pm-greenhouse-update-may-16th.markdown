Yesterday offered a lovely afternoon of activity in the greenhouse. The environment settled into a warm rhythm, with the average temperature reaching a high of 24.13°C by the end of our readings. Light levels were quite active, peaking at 1282.5 lux during the period.

Humidity saw a noticeable dip throughout the afternoon, ranging from a high of 42.9% down to 33.3%. Individual plant temperatures remained quite stable, generally holding in the low to mid-20s Celsius.

Soil moisture levels were consistent across the board. Basil showed a slight dip from its morning peak, while the Chilli and Oregano maintained steady readings throughout the afternoon.

Overall, the conditions appear quite healthy as we wrap up the day. We will continue to monitor the moisture trends as we move into the evening hours to ensure everything remains thriving.
