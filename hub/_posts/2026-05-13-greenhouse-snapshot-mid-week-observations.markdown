Yesterday brought a day of gentle environmental shifts to the Herb Hub 365 greenhouse. The overall atmosphere remained quite stable, with the average temperature holding steady around 19.46°C. Humidity saw a minor dip, starting near 42% and settling closer to 41%.

Light exposure was dynamic throughout the day, building from a low start to a high of 901.7 lux by the final reading. This indicates a solid period of daylight energy for all our herbs. Air pressure also remained steady, fluctuating only slightly between 996 hPa and 997 hPa.

Soil moisture levels showed gradual changes across the plants. Basil maintained a healthy range, dipping from 82.16% to 77.88%. Chilli and Oregano also showed steady trends, with Chilli reaching its lowest reading of 55.83% and Oregano settling at 70.64% by the end of monitoring.

Individual herb temperatures remained consistent, with Basil averaging about 18.13°C and Oregano around 18.12°C. While water reservoir readings were unavailable, the consistent data across all sensors suggests everything is proceeding smoothly for a restful night and a productive day ahead.
