Hello Herb Enthusiasts! Here is your afternoon check-in from the Herb Hub 365 greenhouse, covering the readings from April 20th. Overall, the environment remained quite stable throughout the afternoon hours.

The ambient temperature settled around 22.44°C by the last recorded reading, showing a slight dip from its peak. Humidity levels fluctuated, starting higher in the morning and ending at 33.9%. Light levels also decreased throughout the afternoon, dropping from a high of 1349.2 lux to 361.7 lux by the end of the monitoring period.

For our herbs, the temperatures were consistent across the board. Basil, Chilli, and Oregano all maintained comfortable daytime ranges, and the water reservoir temperature hovered near 20°C.

We observed a slight drying trend in soil moisture, which is typical as the day progresses. Basil's moisture level dropped to 63.54%, while Chilli and Oregano remained in similar ranges at 75.93% and 73.82%, respectively.

Everything appears steady and healthy as we head into the evening. Keep an eye on tomorrow morning's readings for any shifts! Happy growing!
