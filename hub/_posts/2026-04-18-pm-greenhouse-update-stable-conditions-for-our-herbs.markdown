The afternoon readings from the Herb Hub 365 greenhouse show a period of relatively stable conditions as the day wound down. The average temperature held steady throughout the monitoring period, with the recorded highs reaching around 23.91°C.

Humidity dipped slightly, moving from a peak of 42.2% down to 36.1% by the end of the sampling window. Light levels also showed a noticeable decrease, dropping from a high of 2300.8 lux in the morning to 238.3 lux by the afternoon's close.

Soil moisture levels remained consistent for our herbs. Basil maintained a healthy average moisture reading of 87.64%, while Chilli and Oregano held steady around 82% and 79%, respectively.

Temperatures for Basil, Chilli, and Oregano showed gentle fluctuations but remained within comfortable ranges, averaging around 21°C for all three varieties. Everything appears to be settling in nicely for the evening!
