Hello Herb Lovers! We checked in on the greenhouse conditions this afternoon, and everything remained quite stable as the day wound down. The average temperature settled nicely, fluctuating between a low of 17.79°C and a high of 24.14°C during our monitoring period.

Light levels saw a natural dip as the afternoon progressed. Starting at readings up to 2039.2 lux, the intensity steadily decreased, reaching a low of 163.3 lux by the end of the day. Humidity followed a similar trend, moving from around 43.5% down to 36.7%.

Soil moisture also showed a gentle, expected decrease across the board. Basil dipped from 56.65% to 49.37%, while Chilli and Oregano followed similar, gradual declines. Individual herb temperatures remained consistent, with Basil averaging 19.62°C, Chilli around 19.29°C, and Oregano near 19.31°C.

The water reservoir temperature stayed in the mid-to-high teens, suggesting steady conditions for our roots. Overall, the plants seem to be settling into a comfortable rhythm for the evening. We will continue to monitor these readings closely to ensure everything stays optimal for a restful night!
