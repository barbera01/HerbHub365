Hello Herb Gardeners! Here is your afternoon look at the conditions within the Herb Hub 365 greenhouse, covering the period from early morning through midday yesterday. Overall, the environment remained quite stable, offering our herbs a consistent backdrop for growth.

Ambient conditions showed a nice warming trend throughout the monitored hours. Temperatures peaked at 24.58°C, and light levels reached a high of 1018.3 lux, providing plenty of natural energy for our plants. Humidity remained fairly low, fluctuating between a minimum of 31.9% and a maximum of 43.7%.

Soil moisture levels are holding steady across the board. Basil's moisture content saw a slight dip to 78.96%, while Chilli and Oregano remained in similar ranges. These readings suggest the roots are getting the consistent hydration they need.

Individual herb zones also showed nice variation. Oregano reached its highest temperature reading of 21.13°C, while Basil saw its peak at 21.56°C. The water temperature stayed very stable, hovering around 18.69°C. Everything appears to be tracking nicely within healthy parameters. Keep enjoying the vibrant growth—your herbs are thriving!
