Hello Herb Lovers! It’s time for our mid-day check-in from the Herb Hub 365 greenhouse. Our plants have enjoyed a day of consistent monitoring, and the environmental readings show a pleasant pattern as the afternoon progressed.

The ambient temperature remained quite comfortable throughout the afternoon, peaking near 24.51°C around 4:30 PM before settling down to 23.8°C by the end of our readings. Humidity levels also fluctuated, starting higher in the morning and settling around the mid-30s percent by the late afternoon.

Light levels provided plenty of energy for our herbs. We noted the light intensity peaking at 1018.3 lux at one point, though the readings did dip down to 193.3 lux by the final snapshot. Soil moisture remained steady across all our varieties—Basil, Chilli, and Oregano.

Basil’s moisture content ranged from a high of 83.3% down to 75.12%, while Chilli and Oregano maintained levels in the high 70s percent range. Overall, the conditions appear stable and ideal for continued growth. Keep an eye on those moisture levels, as they remain a key indicator of the plants' immediate needs! Happy growing!
